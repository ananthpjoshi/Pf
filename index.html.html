About Our Services




Panchajanya Foundation is a not-for-profit foundation with a goal to create and sustain initiatives to contribute to a just, equitable, humane and sustainable society.

The Foundation’s work is aimed at enriching and enhancing the capabilities of individuals to achieve well-being and harmony with respect to ‘Akshara’ – knowledge, ‘Arogya’ – health and ‘Adhyatma’ – spiritual and moral values.
